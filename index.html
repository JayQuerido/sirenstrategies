Christine started Dossier Communications in 2005, after a decade in publishing. As a communications consultant, she provides a range of services: writing, editing, marketing, event planning, project management. Depending on a client's needs, she brings a team together to get the job done well, on time and on budget. Karen is an accomplished full-time marketing professor at Algonquin College. Official bio

Lori Keith, MBA – For over 30 years she has worked in the public sector and as a consultant, with Aboriginal communities and organizations, provincial health ministries, not-for-profit associations and small businesses in Canada and the U.S. She has developed and delivered courses and workshops on a range of topics. A published author, she has presented her work in Canada, the U.S., Europe and China. She is committed to helping her clients engage creatively and effectively with their clients and communities.
